§ The Club

A full club, kept properly.

Tennis, bowls, a working clubhouse and a steady programme of social play all year round — set on a private, secure site in Dresden, Stoke-on-Trent.

§ 01 — Outdoor

Greens, courts & grounds.

  1. 01

    Secure members' car park

    Key-controlled entry with CCTV throughout. Members and their guests have direct access onto the grounds from a private car park.

  2. 02

    Two floodlit crown-green rinks

    Maintained to a consistent standard for both social and league play, with floodlighting extending the playing hours.

  3. 03

    Four all-weather tennis courts

    Astroturf® surface, floodlit, with meter-controlled lighting and a code-locked gate for members only.

  4. 04

    Sheltered viewing

    Covered spectator areas next to both the greens and the courts — useful on a changeable afternoon.

§ 02 — Inside

Clubhouse, lounge and bar.

  1. 05

    Competitively priced bar

    A proper club bar with sensible prices — open during club sessions, match nights and functions.

  2. 06

    Two snooker tables & table tennis

    Indoor sport available to members through the colder months, in addition to the short-mat bowls setup.

  3. 07

    Lounge & catering

    A comfortable lounge area with catering available for matches, events and private functions.

  4. 08

    Changing rooms & full access

    Ladies' and gents' changing rooms with showers. Disabled access throughout the clubhouse and grounds.

§ 03 — Hire

Available to hire.

Parts of the club can be hired by members and by the public, either for a one-off occasion or on a regular basis. Enquiries go to the club office in the first instance.
