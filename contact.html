§ Contact

Find us, get in touch.

Florence Tennis and Bowling Club is in Dresden, Stoke-on-Trent. Visitors are welcome during club sessions — please ring ahead if you'd like a tour.

§ Visiting

Before you arrive.

  • Visitors are welcome during club opening sessions
  • Ring ahead if you'd like a guided tour or to meet the secretary
  • Members' parking is on site, accessed via Cemetery Avenue
  • Disabled access throughout the clubhouse and grounds
  • The bar opens during club sessions, match nights and functions

Looking to join? The membership page sets out our annual rates and what's included. The secretary is happy to talk anyone through the options before they commit.
