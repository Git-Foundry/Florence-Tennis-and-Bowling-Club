§ Functions

A room for the occasion.

Our function and meeting rooms are available to hire for one-off occasions or on a regular basis — for members and the wider public alike.

§ Suitable for

From quiet meetings to family occasions.

  1. 01

    Birthdays and anniversaries

    A comfortable, characterful space with a fully stocked bar, suitable for milestone celebrations and family gatherings of all sizes.

  2. 02

    Wakes and remembrance

    A respectful, private setting with catering arrangements available, often used by local families for post-funeral receptions.

  3. 03

    Christenings and engagements

    Family-friendly facilities with disabled access throughout and outside space when the weather allows.

  4. 04

    Meetings and clubs

    A separate meeting room is available for community groups, hobby clubs and small business meetings — on a one-off or regular basis.

§ What's included

Everything you'd expect.

  • Function room with seating and tables
  • Separate meeting room for smaller bookings
  • Bar service available throughout
  • Catering arrangements on request
  • Disabled access and parking on site
  • Use of clubhouse facilities and grounds

Members' rates. Club members hire the function and meeting rooms at preferential rates. Non-members are very welcome to enquire — we accept bookings from the wider public throughout the year.
