§ Bowls

Crown-green bowls, well kept.

Two outdoor greens, a short-mat indoor setup for winter and teams entered in the local leagues — social players and competitive bowlers alike find a home here.

§ What's on offer

Social and competitive play.

  1. 01

    Two outdoor greens

    Crown greens maintained to a consistent standard across the season, with sheltered viewing for spectators.

  2. 02

    Short-mat indoor bowls

    A dedicated indoor short-mat setup keeps the game going through the colder months.

  3. 03

    Floodlit play area

    Outdoor floodlighting extends the playing hours into the evening on match and practice nights.

  4. 04

    Teams in local leagues

    Bowlers looking for competitive play will find teams entered in the established local leagues.

  5. 05

    Social play welcomed

    New and returning bowlers are welcome at social sessions — no prior experience needed to turn up and have a go.
