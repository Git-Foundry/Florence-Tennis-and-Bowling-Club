§ Membership

Annual membership, generous terms.

The membership year runs from 1 April to 31 March. Take out tennis, outdoor bowls, indoor bowls — or any combination — at competitive rates with meaningful concessions for seniors and juniors.

§ How to join

A straightforward process.

  1. 01

    Get in touch

    Contact the secretary by phone or email. We're happy to answer questions and arrange a visit before you commit to anything.

  2. 02

    Visit the club

    Have a look round, meet a few members, and try the courts or greens. There is no pressure to sign on the day.

  3. 03

    Complete an application

    A short application form, the appropriate annual subscription, and you're a member. Coaching and league play can be arranged separately.

Contact the secretary Call 01782 319440

§ Notes

Worth knowing.

  • Membership runs annually from 1 April to 31 March
  • Junior membership for under-18s is free, alongside a parent or guardian member
  • Light meter charges apply for floodlit tennis and bowls
  • Coaching is arranged directly with the qualified coach and is paid separately
  • Members may bring guests to use the club, subject to a small visitor fee
  • Function room and meeting room hire is available to members at preferential rates

Concessions. The club offers concessionary rates for over-65s and students in full-time education. Please ask the secretary for the current figures.
