§ 01 — The Club

Two sports, one community.

Florence is a members' club rooted in Dresden, a quiet part of Stoke-on-Trent where the traditions of crown-green bowls and club tennis have always sat comfortably side by side.

The club offers social and competitive play in both sports, with teams entered in local leagues throughout the season. Facilities open year-round — floodlit courts and greens for the long evenings, a short-mat indoor bowls setup for winter, and a lounge and bar that stay warm on match nights.

New members are welcome, whatever the standard. Coaching is on hand for those starting out, and the committee is happy to show anyone around before they commit.

§ 03 — Membership

Competitive rates, generous terms.

Annual membership runs from April to March, with options for tennis, outdoor bowls, indoor bowls, or any combination. Over-65s receive a meaningful concession, and junior membership for under-18s is free.

Full rates, student membership and winter rates are on the membership page. For a quick conversation about what suits you, the secretary can be reached by phone or email.
